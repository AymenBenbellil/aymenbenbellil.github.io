What Operating System do I use?

I use Linux as my main OS, and precisely Arch Linux, even though i use a DEBLOATED Windows 10 too.

Why do did I choose Linux instead of Windows?

  • Has all the tools I need to get work done (like: gcc, vim, mpv, yt-dlp, librewolf, zathura...).
  • No Spyware or telemetry.
  • Easier to use than Windows.
  • Open Source and Free as in Freedom.
  • No bloat.

Why did I choose Arch Linux?

Because it lets me control a big part of my OS unlike other linux distros.
